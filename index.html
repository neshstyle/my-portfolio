Who I Am

I'm Anesh Maharaj — a tech-focused developer and entrepreneur based in Toronto, originally from Trinidad and Tobago. After spending over a decade in Corporate Treasury at Scotiabank, managing liquidity, portfolios, and financial forecasting at an institutional level, I made a deliberate pivot into technology.

I'm currently studying Computer Programming & Analysis at Humber College, sharpening my skills in web development, algorithms, databases, and operating systems. I also run Neshstyle Group Inc., a media production company, and operate NeshAI, an AI automation consulting venture.

My edge: I understand both the business side and the technical side. I don't just build things — I build things that matter.
